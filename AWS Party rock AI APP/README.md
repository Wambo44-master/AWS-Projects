# StudyFlow: Learn Smarter Faster

## 📋 Project Overview

**StudyFlow: Learn Smarter Faster** is a Generative AI application built using **AWS PartyRock**, the Amazon Bedrock playground. It was developed as part of the **AWS AI and ML Challenge** to demonstrate practical AI skills in building intelligent, user-focused applications.

**Live App:** [https://partyrock.aws/u/Nicole998/-M3bOMTgU/StudyFlow%253A-Learn-Smarter-Faster](https://partyrock.aws/u/Nicole998/-M3bOMTgU/StudyFlow%253A-Learn-Smarter-Faster)

## 🎯 Use Case

StudyFlow helps students and lifelong learners optimize their study sessions by generating personalized study plans, recommending effective learning techniques, and creating self-testing questions.

**Who is this for?**
- Students preparing for exams
- Professionals learning new skills
- Anyone who wants to study more effectively

## 🚀 How It Works

The app uses a simple, user-friendly interface where you provide three inputs:

| Input Field | What It Does |
|-------------|--------------|
| **Subject or Topic** | Enter what you are studying (e.g., "AWS Cloud Practitioner", "Linear Algebra", "French Grammar") |
| **Time Available** | Specify how much time you have for your study session |
| **Current Knowledge Level** | Optional: Share your current understanding of the topic |

Based on your inputs, the app generates three outputs:

| Output | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Recommended Study Technique** | Suggests the most effective learning method for your situation (e.g., Pomodoro, Spaced Repetition, Active Recall) |
| **Personalized Study Plan** | Provides a minute-by-minute breakdown of how to structure your study session |
| **Key Concepts and Questions** | Lists essential concepts to focus on and self-testing questions to verify your understanding |
